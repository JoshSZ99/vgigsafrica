How we vet Talent

Technical Assessment


In this phase, a testing tool is used to assess the candidate's technical and communication skills. Candidate's technical knowledge is assessed on their performance on a string of tasks centered on their preferred programming language data structure and algorithm proficiency.



Recruiter Interview


Utilizing video conferencing, candidates are required to go through a recruiter interview where we further assess their hard skills and soft skills. On succeeding through the vetting stages, the candidate is approved for placements.
